# Digital-Marketing
Digital marketing concepts.

Digital marketing refers to the use of digital channels, platforms, and technologies to reach audiences, share information, and engage with customers online. It is a broad approach that integrates multiple tactics to help organizations connect with people where they spend much of their time - on the internet, social media, and mobile devices

Common digital marketing practices include content creation, search engine optimization (SEO), social media engagement, email communication, mobile outreach, and display advertising. These techniques help distribute relevant messages through blogs, websites, search results, and social networks to attract attention and support measurable outcomes.This overview is informed by insights available on this [technology platform ](https://www.striveontech.com/services/digital-marketing/), which highlights common approaches to digital marketing.

Digital marketing is increasingly essential in modern business environments because it offers a data-driven way to understand user behavior, adjust strategies, and potentially improve visibility and engagement across online audiences. Its cost-effective nature allows both small and large entities to adopt digital methods as part of broader marketing plans.

Overall, digital marketing supports online presence by leveraging technology, analytics, and diverse digital touchpoints to reach and interact with target audiences in efficient, trackable ways.
